# Upgrade or Patch the VCF Management Components

Use VCF Operations to upgrade or patch the VCF management components.

Before upgrading or patching the VCF management components, you must generate a download token and must configure and connect to an offline or an online depot.

You then download the required binaries and upgrade or patch the VCF Operations fleet management appliance to the respective compatible version. VCF Operations fleet management appliance must be upgraded or patched before you can upgrade or patch the remaining VCF management components.
