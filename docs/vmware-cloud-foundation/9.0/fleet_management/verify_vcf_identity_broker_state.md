# Verify VCF Identity Broker State

After performing a restore of VCF Identity Broker, you verify its state.

1. Log in to the VCF Operations interface at https://<vcf\_operations\_fqdn> as a user assigned Administrator role.
2. Navigate to Fleet ManagementLifecycleVCF ManagementComponents.
3. Under components, click VCF Identity Broker.
4. On the component summary page, click the horizontal elipsis and click Trigger inventory Sync.
5. On the Trigger inventory Sync page, click Submit.
6. Monitor the inventory sync task on the Tasks tab.
