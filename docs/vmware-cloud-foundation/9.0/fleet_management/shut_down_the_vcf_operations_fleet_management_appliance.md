# Shut Down the VCF Operations Fleet Management Appliance

Shut down the VCF Operations fleet management appliance in the management domain of VCF Instance hosting the VCF from the vSphere Client.

1. Log in to vCenter for the management domain at https://<vcenter\_fqdn>/ui as a user with the Administrator role.
2. In the VMs and Templates inventory, locate the VCF Operations fleet management appliance.
3. Right-click the VCF Operations fleet management appliance and select PowerShut down Guest OS.
4. In the confirmation dialog box, click Yes.
