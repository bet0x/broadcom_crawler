# vSphere Supervisor Zone Models

vSphere Supervisor management workloads can be deployed to one or three management zones. Workloads in vSphere Namespaces can be deployed to the same or separate dedicated zones to isolate them from management workloads. This section details the different deployment models to choose from.

- [Single Management Zone with Combined Workload Zones Model](/us/en/vmware-cis/vcf/vcf-9-0-and-later/9-0/design/design-library/self-service-iaas-deployment-models/vsphere-supervisor-zone-models/single-zone.html)
- [Single Management Zone with Isolated Workload Zones Model](/us/en/vmware-cis/vcf/vcf-9-0-and-later/9-0/design/design-library/self-service-iaas-deployment-models/vsphere-supervisor-zone-models/three-zone.html)
- [Three Management Zones with Combined Workload Zones Model](/us/en/vmware-cis/vcf/vcf-9-0-and-later/9-0/design/design-library/self-service-iaas-deployment-models/vsphere-supervisor-zone-models/three-management-zones-with-combined-workload-zones-model.html)
- [Three Management Zones with Isolated Workload Zones Model](/us/en/vmware-cis/vcf/vcf-9-0-and-later/9-0/design/design-library/self-service-iaas-deployment-models/vsphere-supervisor-zone-models/three-management-zones-with-isolated-workload-zones-model.html)
