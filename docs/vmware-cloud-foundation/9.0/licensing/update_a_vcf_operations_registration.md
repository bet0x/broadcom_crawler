# Update a VCF Operations Registration

If a change is made to your environment and it affects your ability to update your licenses, you must update the registration of the VCF Operations instance with the VCF Business Services console.

When this change occurs, a banner appears on top of the screen and requires you to update the VCF Operations registration. To be able to continue updating or importing your licenses in this VCF Operations instance, you must first update the registration.

1. In the banner, click Generate Updated Registration File.
2. Download the registration file.
3. Log in to the VCF Business Services console.
4. Navigate to License ManagementVCF Operations Registrations.
5. Click the vertical ellipsis next to the VCF Operations instance that you must update.
6. Click Upload Updated Registration File.

   A dialog box appears.
7. In the Upload Updated Registration File dialog box, click Browse and upload the file that you downloaded from your VCF Operations instance.
8. Select the attestation check box, and click Import.
9. Click Close.

The registration of the VCF Operations instance is updated. You can manage the licenses added to the instance.
